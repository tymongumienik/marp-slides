### Method

<div style="margin-top: 4px;">
  <div style="display: flex; gap: 10px; margin-bottom: 14px;">
    <div style="width: 24px; height: 24px; border-radius: 50%; border: 1px solid #c4956a; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: center; font-family: 'DM Serif Display'; font-size: 0.7em; color: #c4956a; flex-shrink: 0;">1</div>
    <div>
      <div style="font-weight: 400; font-size: 0.72em; color: #f5f0eb;">Mix the glaze</div>
      <div style="font-size: 0.6em; color: #5a5048; margin-top: 1px;">Whisk miso, mirin, soy sauce, sesame oil. Coat salmon. Rest 10 min.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div style="display: flex; gap: 10px; margin-bottom: 14px;">
    <div style="width: 24px; height: 24px; border-radius: 50%; border: 1px solid #c4956a; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: center; font-family: 'DM Serif Display'; font-size: 0.7em; color: #c4956a; flex-shrink: 0;">2</div>
    <div>
      <div style="font-weight: 400; font-size: 0.72em; color: #f5f0eb;">Cook the rice</div>
      <div style="font-size: 0.6em; color: #5a5048; margin-top: 1px;">Rinse jasmine rice, cook per package. Fold in sesame seeds.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div style="display: flex; gap: 10px; margin-bottom: 14px;">
    <div style="width: 24px; height: 24px; border-radius: 50%; border: 1px solid #c4956a; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: center; font-family: 'DM Serif Display'; font-size: 0.7em; color: #c4956a; flex-shrink: 0;">3</div>
    <div>
      <div style="font-weight: 400; font-size: 0.72em; color: #f5f0eb;">Quick pickle</div>
      <div style="font-size: 0.6em; color: #5a5048; margin-top: 1px;">Cucumber + rice vinegar, pinch of sugar and salt.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div style="display: flex; gap: 10px; margin-bottom: 14px;">
    <div style="width: 24px; height: 24px; border-radius: 50%; border: 1px solid #c4956a; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: center; font-family: 'DM Serif Display'; font-size: 0.7em; color: #c4956a; flex-shrink: 0;">4</div>
    <div>
      <div style="font-weight: 400; font-size: 0.72em; color: #f5f0eb;">Sear the salmon</div>
      <div style="font-size: 0.6em; color: #5a5048; margin-top: 1px;">Skin down 4 min, flip 3 min. Let the glaze caramelize.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div style="display: flex; gap: 10px;">
    <div style="width: 24px; height: 24px; border-radius: 50%; background: #c4956a; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: center; font-family: 'DM Serif Display'; font-size: 0.7em; color: #0c0a08; flex-shrink: 0;">5</div>
    <div>
      <div style="font-weight: 400; font-size: 0.72em; color: #f5f0eb;">Plate</div>
      <div style="font-size: 0.6em; color: #5a5048; margin-top: 1px;">Rice, salmon on top, cucumber alongside. Scatter scallions.</div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
